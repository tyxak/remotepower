# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome contributions from everyone regardless of background
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the repo owner via email (see SECURITY.md).
Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or ban contributors.
